## 🚨 Common Issues to Watch For

### Technical Issues
- **Slow Loading Times** - Monitor and optimize regularly
- **Mobile Usability** - Test on various devices
- **Crawl Errors** - Fix 404s and server errors promptly
- **Duplicate Content** - Ensure canonical tags are working
- **Missing Meta Tags** - Regular audits for new pages

### Content Issues
- **Thin Content** - Ensure all pages have substantial, valuable content
- **Keyword Cannibalization** - Avoid targeting same keywords on multiple pages
- **Outdated Information** - Keep business hours, prices, and services current
- **Missing Alt Text** - Ensure all images have descriptive alt attributes
